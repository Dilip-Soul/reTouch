About reTouch:


At reTouch, we are dedicated to revolutionizing waste management by seamlessly connecting waste pickers with recycling companies. Our mission is to empower waste pickers with innovative tools, such as camera-enabled devices and location-based systems, to streamline the collection and sale of recyclable materials. By facilitating direct transactions between waste pickers and recycling companies, we ensure fair compensation and promote sustainable practices. With a modest 5% commission on each deal, we reinvest in our platform to continually enhance services, fostering a cleaner environment and a more equitable waste management ecosystem.

Our Services at reTouch:

At reTouch, we offer a suite of services designed to streamline waste management processes, connecting waste pickers directly with recycling companies to foster a sustainable environment. Our key offerings include:​
1. Waste Picker Empowerment Tools: We provide waste pickers with innovative tools, including camera-enabled devices and location-based systems, to enhance efficiency in waste collection and sales.​

2. Direct Transaction Facilitation: Our platform enables seamless transactions between waste pickers and recycling companies, ensuring fair compensation and promoting transparency in the waste management process.​

3. Minimal Commission Structure: reTouch operates with a modest 5% commission on each transaction, reinvesting in our platform to continually enhance services and support our users.​

4. Educational Resources and Support: We offer training and resources to waste pickers, equipping them with knowledge and skills to maximize earnings and contribute effectively to environmental sustainability.​

5. Data Analytics for Recycling Companies: Our platform provides recycling companies with valuable data insights, aiding in optimizing operations and making informed decisions in waste material procurement.​ By choosing reTouch, you partner with a company committed to innovation, sustainability, and equitable growth in the waste management sector.
